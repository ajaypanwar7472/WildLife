WildLife Journey

Wildlife encompasses all living organisms that inhabit natural environments, excluding domesticated animals and cultivated plants. It includes a vast array of species ranging from microscopic organisms to large mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and insects. Wildlife plays crucial roles in ecosystems, such as pollination, seed dispersal, pest control, and nutrient cycling.

Legal Protection and Public Awareness

Laws and regulations at local, national, and international levels aim to protect wildlife and their habitats, including the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species (CITES) and the Endangered Species Act (ESA).

Education and outreach programs raise awareness about the importance of wildlife conservation and encourage sustainable practices to reduce human impact on natural ecosystems.
